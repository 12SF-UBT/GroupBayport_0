Who we are
Meet Group
Bayport
We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of customized and personalized products, including signages, displays, covers, tarps, home decor, and lifestyle items.

Alongside our mass production capabilities, we run several e-commerce platforms offering a wide selection of high-quality products.

Group Bayport operates as Avaan International, Inc. (US) and Caterpillar Signs Private Limited (India), headquartered in Georgia, Atlanta USA. Our manufacturing & operations facilities are in Atlanta, Phoenix, and India, with the US being our largest market.
